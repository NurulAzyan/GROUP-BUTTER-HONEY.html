About Us

Our BUTTER HONEY represent something really sweet but it is also containing healthy ingredient such as original honey named “Madu Kelulut”. The business was open and established on 1 June 2019. The main desserts are Turkish Delights, Beignets and Brownies. These three desserts are contained with butter and honey as the main ingredients and it can be keep and stored for a long period of time. We want to show our customer the high quality and tasty dessert made by us.

"there is nothing better than a butter, unless it is a friend with honey."
